Coronavirus - travel from one area to another

{{ govukInsetText({ html: 'There are different restrictions in Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.' }) }}

Use this to find out the rules you need to follow when you’re visiting somewhere else in England, for example if you're visiting a friend or relative or going shopping in another town. If you’re a student returning home from university there are different rules.

There’s a different service to find out what rules to follow where you’re living

When you’re in another place, and on the way there, the rules you need to follow depend on:

  • the Local COVID Alert Level where you’re living or spend most of your time
  • the level of the place you’re going to.

When you’re returning from somewhere you’ve been, then you need to follow the rules for where you’re living.

You might not be allowed to visit some areas unless you’re making an essential journey.
